About Our Club

Our Mission

To foster innovation and technical skills among students through hands-on robotics projects and competitions, preparing them for future challenges in technology and engineering.

Our History

Founded in 2081 by a group of passionate engineering students, we've grown from 1 members to over 10 active participants. Our club has won numerous national competitions and helped launch careers in robotics and AI.
